Ananya Mittal

@ananyaontheshor

I write things.

I build things.

Sometimes the things I build become the things I write about, and sometimes the writing explains why I built them. The line between the two is blurry and I've stopped trying to find it.

I'm interested in how systems work and what happens to people inside them. I've studied law, learned to read data, built tools that try to make inaccessible things legible. I'm not sure those are three different things.

I game. I paint sometimes. I pick up hobbies and abandon them with great enthusiasm. I've been writing poetry longer than I've been doing anything else, which probably explains something about me that I haven't figured out yet.

This page is called ananyaontheshor. Shor means noise. It also means shore. I've been thinking about that for a while.

I'm building this for me.
