right now (a work in progress)

jun 2026

I finished my MBA with 58%. 4.64 out of 8.

I'm going to say that plainly because I've spent enough time being ashamed of it. Stats was hard. Finance was harder. The placements didn't come. At some point I stopped being able to study, stopped being able to focus, stopped being able to do much of anything except exist in a state of low-grade dread and wait for something to change.

Nothing changed for a while.

The job search is still ongoing. Some days it feels like everyone can see something wrong with me that I can't quite identify. Too creative for the analytical roles, too analytical for the creative ones, too much law for the tech people, too much tech for the law people. I don't fit cleanly anywhere and the world, it turns out, really likes clean fits.

So I decided to stop waiting for permission and just build things.

This website exists because of that decision. NyayaAI exists because of that decision. The essays on this page exist because one night I sat down and refused to do anything that wasn't for me.

I'll be honest about something. I didn't build all of this alone. I used tools, asked for help, learned as I went. But the ideas were mine. The problem NyayaAI solves is one I understood because of five years of law. Using tools doesn't disqualify the work. Every builder uses tools.

I'm still scared sometimes that doing this is wasting time I should spend on applications and interview prep. That making things I love is somehow irresponsible.

But I've noticed something. When I'm building, my brain works. The same brain that went grey and quiet through law school and parts of the MBA comes alive when there's a problem to solve and the freedom to solve it my way.

I don't fully understand what my life is yet. But I'm starting to think that's okay.

I'm building this for me. Everything else can wait.
